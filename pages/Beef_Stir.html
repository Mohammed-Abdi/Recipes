Instructions

1. Marinate the Beef
In a bowl, combine thinly sliced beef with soy sauce, oyster sauce, and cornstarch. Mix well and let it marinate for 15 minutes.
2. Prepare the Vegetables
Slice the bell peppers, julienne the carrot, and cut the broccoli into florets. Mince the garlic and ginger.
3. Cook the Beef
Heat 1 tbsp vegetable oil in a wok or large skillet over high heat. Add the marinated beef and stir-fry for 2-3 minutes until browned. Remove and set aside.
4. Stir-Fry the Vegetables
In the same wok, add the remaining oil. Stir-fry the garlic and ginger for 30 seconds, then add the vegetables. Cook for 3-4 minutes until tender-crisp.
5. Combine and Serve
Return the beef to the wok. Add beef broth and stir-fry for 2-3 minutes until the sauce thickens. Serve hot with steamed rice.
