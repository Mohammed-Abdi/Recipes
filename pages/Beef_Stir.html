Beef Stir Fry

PREP TIME15 Minutes
COOK TIME15 Minutes
Meat

Nutrition Information

Calories 300 kcal
Total Fat 12 g
Protein 25 g
Carbohydrate 20 g
Cholesterol 60 mg

Beef Stir Fry is a quick and flavorful dish featuring tender slices of beef, crisp vegetables, and a savory sauce. This dish is perfect for busy weeknights, as it comes together in just 30 minutes. Serve it over steamed rice or noodles for a complete and satisfying meal.

Ingredients













Instructions

1. Prepare the Sauce
In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, oyster sauce, cornstarch, and black pepper. Set aside.
2. Cook the Beef
Heat 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over high heat. Add the beef slices and stir-fry for 2-3 minutes until browned. Remove the beef from the skillet and set aside.
3. Stir-Fry the Vegetables
In the same skillet, add the remaining 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil. Add the minced garlic and ginger, and sauté for 30 seconds until fragrant. Add the bell peppers, broccoli, and carrot, and stir-fry for 3-4 minutes until the vegetables are tender-crisp.
4. Combine Beef and Sauce
Return the beef to the skillet with the vegetables. Pour the sauce over the beef and vegetables, and stir-fry for 1-2 minutes until everything is well-coated and heated through.
5. Serve
Serve the Beef Stir Fry hot over steamed rice or noodles. Garnish with sesame seeds or chopped green onions if desired.
