German Soft Pretzel

PREP TIME20 Minutes
COOK TIME25 Minutes
Snack

Protein 5 g
Carbohydrate 35 g

A classic German snack, soft pretzels are golden-brown on the outside and fluffy on the inside. With a sprinkle of coarse salt, they are perfect as an appetizer, snack, or side dish. Making these pretzels at home brings the taste of Germany right to your kitchen!

Instructions

1. Prepare the Yeast Mixture
In a small bowl, mix the warm water, sugar, and yeast. Let it sit for 5-10 minutes, until the yeast becomes foamy.
2. Make the Dough
In a large bowl, combine the flour and salt. Add the yeast mixture and olive oil. Stir until a dough forms. Knead the dough on a lightly floured surface for about 5-7 minutes, until smooth.
3. Let the Dough Rise
Place the dough in a greased bowl and cover with a clean towel. Let it rise in a warm place for 1 hour, or until it has doubled in size.
4. Shape the Pretzels
Preheat the oven to 450°F (230°C). Punch down the dough and divide it into 8 equal portions. Roll each portion into a long rope, then twist each rope into a pretzel shape.
5. Boil the Pretzels
Bring a large pot of water to a boil, and add the baking soda. Carefully lower the pretzels into the water and boil for 30 seconds. Remove and place on a greased baking sheet.
6. Bake
Sprinkle the pretzels with coarse salt and bake in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until golden brown.
7. Serve
Allow the pretzels to cool slightly before serving. Enjoy them warm, with mustard or your favorite dip.
