Banana Bread

COOK TIME1 Hour
Dessert

Banana bread is a sweet, moist, and fluffy quick bread made with ripe bananas. It's an easy recipe and a perfect way to use up overripe bananas. A deliciously comforting dessert, perfect with a cup of tea or coffee.

Instructions

1. Prepare the Bananas
Peel the bananas and place them in a large mixing bowl. Mash the bananas using a fork or potato masher until smooth, leaving a few lumps for texture.
2. Mix Wet Ingredients
In the same bowl, add the melted butter, sugar, beaten eggs, and vanilla extract. Mix everything together until well combined.
3. Mix Dry Ingredients
In a separate bowl, whisk together the flour, baking soda, and salt. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, stirring until just combined.
4. Add Optional Ingredients
If you're adding walnuts or chocolate chips, fold them into the batter at this point.
5. Bake the Banana Bread
Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Pour the batter into a greased 9x5-inch loaf pan. Bake for 60-65 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
6. Cool and Serve
Allow the banana bread to cool in the pan for about 10 minutes before transferring it to a wire rack to cool completely. Slice and enjoy!
