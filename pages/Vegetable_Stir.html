Vegetable Stir Fry is a quick, healthy, and flavorful dish packed with colorful vegetables and a savory soy sauce-based sauce. Perfect for a light lunch or dinner, this dish is easy to customize with your favorite veggies. Serve it over rice or noodles for a complete meal!

Instructions

1. Prepare the Vegetables
Wash and chop all the vegetables into bite-sized pieces. Set aside.
2. Heat the Oil
Heat the vegetable oil and sesame oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add the minced garlic and grated ginger, and sautÃ© for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
3. Stir Fry the Vegetables
Add the broccoli, bell peppers, carrot, and snap peas to the skillet. Stir fry for 5-7 minutes until the vegetables are tender but still crisp.
4. Add the Sauce
Pour the soy sauce over the vegetables and toss to coat. Cook for an additional 1-2 minutes until everything is well combined and heated through.
5. Serve
Serve the vegetable stir fry hot over steamed rice or noodles. Garnish with sesame seeds or chopped green onions if desired.
