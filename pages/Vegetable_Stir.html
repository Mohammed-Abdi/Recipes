Vegetable Stir Fry

PREP TIME15 Minutes
COOK TIME10 Minutes
Vegetarian

Nutrition Information

Calories 150 kcal
Total Fat 6 g
Protein 4 g
Carbohydrate 20 g
Cholesterol 0 mg

Vegetable Stir Fry is a quick, healthy, and flavorful dish packed with colorful vegetables and a savory sauce. Perfect for a light lunch or dinner, this stir fry is easy to customize with your favorite veggies and can be served over rice or noodles for a complete meal.

Ingredients












Instructions

1. Prepare the Sauce
In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, oyster sauce, and sesame oil. Set aside.
2. Heat the Pan
Heat a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add a drizzle of sesame oil.
3. Stir-Fry the Vegetables
Add the minced garlic and ginger to the skillet and sauté for 30 seconds until fragrant. Add the bell peppers, broccoli, carrot, and snap peas. Stir-fry for 4-5 minutes until the vegetables are tender-crisp.
4. Add the Sauce
Pour the prepared sauce over the vegetables and toss to coat. Cook for an additional 1-2 minutes until everything is heated through.
5. Serve
Serve the Vegetable Stir Fry hot over steamed rice or noodles. Garnish with sesame seeds or chopped green onions if desired.
