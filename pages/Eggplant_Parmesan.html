Eggplant Parmesan

PREP TIME15 Minutes
COOK TIME30 Minutes

Eggplant Parmesan is a classic Italian dish featuring breaded and fried eggplant slices layered with marinara sauce and melted cheese. It's a deliciously hearty and comforting meal that can be served as a main or side dish.

Instructions

1. Prepare the Eggplant
Slice the eggplants into 1/4 inch rounds. Sprinkle both sides with salt and set aside for 10 minutes to draw out excess moisture. Pat dry with a paper towel.
2. Bread the Eggplant
Dip the eggplant slices into the beaten eggs, then coat with breadcrumbs and grated Parmesan cheese. Repeat for all slices.
3. Fry the Eggplant
Heat olive oil in a large frying pan over medium heat. Fry the breaded eggplant slices for about 2-3 minutes per side, until golden and crispy. Set aside on a paper towel to drain excess oil.
4. Layer the Eggplant
Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). In a baking dish, spread a thin layer of marinara sauce, then place a layer of fried eggplant slices. Top with marinara sauce and a generous amount of mozzarella and Parmesan cheese. Repeat with the remaining ingredients, finishing with a layer of cheese on top.
5. Bake
Place the dish in the preheated oven and bake for 20-25 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
6. Serve
Let the dish cool for a few minutes before serving. Garnish with fresh basil leaves, if desired. Serve with pasta or a side salad for a complete meal.
