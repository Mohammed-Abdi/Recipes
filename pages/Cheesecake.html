Cheesecake

PREP TIME20 Minutes
COOK TIME55 Minutes
Dessert

Nutrition Information

Calories 320 kcal
Total Fat 22 g
Protein 6 g
Carbohydrate 28 g
Cholesterol 80 mg

This creamy and rich homemade cheesecake is a classic dessert that is easy to make. A buttery graham cracker crust is filled with a smooth cream cheese mixture, then baked to perfection.

Ingredients







Instructions

1. Preheat the Oven
Preheat your oven to 325°F (163°C).
2. Prepare the Crust
Mix graham cracker crumbs and melted butter, then press into the bottom of a springform pan.
3. Make the Filling
Beat cream cheese and sugar until smooth. Add eggs one at a time, then stir in vanilla.
4. Bake
Pour the filling over the crust and bake for 50-55 minutes, or until the center is set.
5. Cool and Serve
Let the cheesecake cool, then refrigerate for at least 4 hours before serving.
