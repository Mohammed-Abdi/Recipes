Cheesecake

COOK TIME1 Hour
Dessert

A classic cheesecake that’s rich and creamy, with a crisp graham cracker crust and a smooth, tangy filling. This dessert is a perfect balance of flavors, ideal for any occasion. You can top it with fresh fruit, chocolate ganache, or even caramel for extra sweetness.

Instructions

1. Prepare the Crust
Mix the graham cracker crumbs, sugar, and melted butter in a bowl. Press the mixture into the bottom of a springform pan.
2. Bake the Crust
Bake the crust in a preheated 325°F (160°C) oven for 10 minutes. Remove and cool.
3. Prepare the Filling
Beat the cream cheese and sugar until smooth. Add the eggs, vanilla, sour cream, and heavy cream, mixing until fully combined.
4. Bake the Cheesecake
Pour the filling over the crust and bake for 50-60 minutes until the center is just set. Let it cool completely.
5. Chill the Cheesecake
Refrigerate the cheesecake for at least 4 hours or overnight to allow it to firm up before serving.
