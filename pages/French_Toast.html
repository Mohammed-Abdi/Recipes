Cinnamon French Toast

PREP TIME10 Minutes
COOK TIME15 Minutes
Breakfast

Nutrition Information

Total Fat 8 g
Protein 10 g
Carbohydrate 35 g
Cholesterol 150 mg

Thick slices of bread soaked in a rich cinnamon-vanilla custard, cooked to golden perfection. A classic breakfast dish that’s crispy on the outside and custardy on the inside.

Instructions

1. Prepare the Custard
In a shallow bowl, whisk together eggs, milk, vanilla extract, and cinnamon until smooth.
2. Soak the Bread
Dip each slice of bread into the custard mixture, allowing it to soak for 20-30 seconds per side.
3. Cook the Toast
Melt butter in a skillet over medium heat. Cook the soaked bread for 3-4 minutes per side until golden brown.
4. Serve
Serve warm with maple syrup, fresh berries, or a dusting of powdered sugar.
