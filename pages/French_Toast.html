French Toast

PREP TIME10 Minutes
COOK TIME10 Minutes
Breakfast

Nutrition Information

Calories 250 kcal
Total Fat 8 g
Protein 10 g
Carbohydrate 35 g
Cholesterol 120 mg

French Toast is a classic breakfast dish made with slices of bread soaked in a mixture of eggs, milk, and cinnamon, then fried to golden perfection. Serve it with maple syrup, fresh fruit, or a dusting of powdered sugar for a delicious start to your day.

Ingredients








Instructions

1. Prepare the Egg Mixture
In a shallow dish, whisk together the eggs, milk, vanilla extract, and cinnamon.
2. Soak the Bread
Dip each slice of bread into the egg mixture, ensuring both sides are well-coated.
3. Cook the French Toast
Heat a skillet over medium heat and add the butter. Cook the bread slices for 2-3 minutes on each side, or until golden brown.
4. Serve
Serve the French Toast warm with maple syrup, fresh fruit, or a dusting of powdered sugar.
