Roasted Vegetables

COOK TIME30 Minutes
Roasted

Roasted vegetables are a perfect side dish for any meal. The combination of seasonal vegetables, tossed in olive oil and spices, creates a flavorful, crispy exterior and a tender interior.

Instructions

1. Preheat the Oven
Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C). This ensures that the vegetables roast evenly and get a nice crispy texture.
2. Prepare the Vegetables
Wash and peel (if necessary) the vegetables. Cut them into bite-sized pieces and place them on a baking sheet.
3. Season the Vegetables
Drizzle olive oil over the vegetables, then sprinkle with dried oregano, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Toss to coat evenly.
4. Roast the Vegetables
Roast the vegetables for 25-30 minutes, stirring halfway through, until they are golden brown and tender.
5. Serve
Remove the vegetables from the oven and serve hot as a side dish or mixed with your favorite grain or protein.
