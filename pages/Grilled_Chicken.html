Grilled Chicken with Herb Marinade

PREP TIME10 Minutes
COOK TIME15 Minutes
Meat

Nutrition Information

Calories 220 kcal
Total Fat 8 g
Protein 35 g
Carbohydrate 2 g
Cholesterol 85 mg

Grilled Chicken with Herb Marinade is a flavorful and healthy dish featuring tender chicken breasts marinated in a blend of fresh herbs, garlic, olive oil, and lemon juice. Grilled to perfection, this dish is juicy, aromatic, and perfect for a quick weeknight dinner or a weekend barbecue. Serve it with a side of roasted vegetables or a fresh salad for a complete meal.

Ingredients










Instructions

1. Prepare the Marinade
In a small bowl, combine the olive oil, minced garlic, chopped rosemary, thyme, parsley, lemon juice, salt, and black pepper. Mix well to create a flavorful marinade.
2. Marinate the Chicken
Place the chicken breasts in a large resealable plastic bag or a shallow dish. Pour the marinade over the chicken, ensuring each piece is evenly coated. Seal the bag or cover the dish and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes, or up to 4 hours for deeper flavor.
3. Preheat the Grill
Preheat your grill to medium-high heat (about 375°F to 400°F or 190°C to 200°C). If using a grill pan, preheat it on the stovetop over medium-high heat.
4. Grill the Chicken
Remove the chicken from the marinade, shaking off any excess. Place the chicken breasts on the preheated grill and cook for 6-7 minutes per side, or until the internal temperature reaches 165°F (75°C) and the chicken is golden brown with grill marks.
5. Rest and Serve
Transfer the grilled chicken to a plate and let it rest for 5 minutes to allow the juices to redistribute. Garnish with additional fresh herbs if desired, and serve with your favorite sides.
