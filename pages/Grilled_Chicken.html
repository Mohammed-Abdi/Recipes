Instructions

1. Prepare the Marinade
In a bowl, combine olive oil, minced garlic, rosemary, thyme, salt, pepper, and lemon juice. Whisk until well blended.
2. Marinate the Chicken
Place chicken breasts in a resealable bag or shallow dish. Pour the marinade over the chicken, ensuring it's fully coated. Refrigerate for at least 1 hour, or overnight for best results.
3. Preheat the Grill
Preheat your grill to medium-high heat (about 400Â°F or 200Â°C). Lightly oil the grates to prevent sticking.
4. Grill the Chicken
Remove chicken from the marinade, shaking off excess. Place on the grill and cook for 6-7 minutes per side, or until the internal temperature reaches 165Â°F (75Â°C).
5. Rest and Serve
Let the chicken rest for 5 minutes before slicing. Serve with a side of grilled vegetables or a fresh salad.
