Grilled Chicken with Herb Marinade

COOK TIME15 Minutes

Grilled Chicken with Herb Marinade is a flavorful and juicy dish featuring tender chicken breasts marinated in a blend of fresh herbs, olive oil, and garlic. Perfectly grilled to perfection, this dish is a healthy and delicious option for any meal. Serve it with a side of vegetables or a fresh salad for a complete meal!

Instructions

1. Prepare the Marinade
In a small bowl, combine the olive oil, minced garlic, rosemary, thyme, parsley, salt, black pepper, and lemon juice. Mix well to create the herb marinade.
2. Marinate the Chicken
Place the chicken breasts in a large resealable bag or shallow dish. Pour the marinade over the chicken, ensuring each piece is well-coated. Seal the bag or cover the dish and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes, or up to 4 hours for deeper flavor.
3. Preheat the Grill
Preheat your grill to medium-high heat (about 375Â°F to 400Â°F or 190Â°C to 200Â°C). If using a grill pan, preheat it on the stovetop over medium-high heat.
4. Grill the Chicken
Remove the chicken from the marinade and shake off any excess. Place the chicken on the preheated grill and cook for 6-7 minutes per side, or until the internal temperature reaches 165Â°F (75Â°C) and the chicken is golden brown with grill marks.
5. Rest and Serve
Transfer the grilled chicken to a plate and let it rest for 5 minutes. Serve with a side of grilled vegetables, a fresh salad, or your favorite side dish.
