Instructions

1. Prepare the Sauce
In a large pan, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and garlic, and cook until softened, about 5 minutes. Add the ground beef and cook until browned, breaking it apart with a spoon.
2. Add Tomatoes and Seasonings
Stir in the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, oregano, sugar, salt, and pepper. Reduce the heat and let the sauce simmer for about 30 minutes, stirring occasionally, until thickened.
3. Cook the Spaghetti
While the sauce simmers, cook the spaghetti according to package instructions. Drain the pasta and set aside, reserving some pasta water for later use.
4. Combine the Pasta and Sauce
Add the cooked spaghetti to the sauce, tossing to coat the pasta. If needed, add a bit of reserved pasta water to reach the desired consistency.
5. Serve
Divide the spaghetti between serving plates and garnish with fresh basil. Serve with grated Parmesan cheese if desired.
