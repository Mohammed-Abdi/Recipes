Spaghetti Bolognese

PREP TIME15 Minutes
COOK TIME30 Minutes
Meat

Nutrition Information

Calories 450 kcal

Protein 25 g
Carbohydrate 45 g

Spaghetti Bolognese is a classic Italian dish featuring al dente spaghetti topped with a rich and savory meat sauce. The sauce is made with ground beef, tomatoes, onions, garlic, and herbs, creating a hearty and flavorful meal. Perfect for family dinners or special occasions, this dish is a crowd-pleaser!

Instructions

1. Cook the Spaghetti
Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the spaghetti and cook according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
2. Prepare the Sauce
In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, and sauté until softened and fragrant, about 3-4 minutes.
3. Cook the Beef
Add the ground beef to the skillet and cook until browned, breaking it up with a spoon as it cooks. Drain any excess fat.
4. Add Tomatoes and Seasonings
Stir in the canned tomatoes, tomato paste, dried oregano, dried basil, salt, and pepper. Simmer the sauce for 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally, until it thickens.
5. Combine and Serve
Add the cooked spaghetti to the skillet and toss to coat it with the sauce. Serve hot, garnished with grated Parmesan cheese.
