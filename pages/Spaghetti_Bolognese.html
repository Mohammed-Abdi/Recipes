Spaghetti Bolognese

PREP TIME15 Minutes
COOK TIME30 Minutes
Meat

Nutrition Information

Calories 450 kcal
Total Fat 15 g
Protein 25 g
Carbohydrate 55 g
Cholesterol 60 mg

Spaghetti Bolognese is a classic Italian dish featuring al dente spaghetti topped with a rich and savory meat sauce made from ground beef, tomatoes, and aromatic herbs. This hearty and comforting meal is perfect for family dinners or special occasions. Serve it with a sprinkle of Parmesan cheese and fresh basil for an extra touch of flavor.

Ingredients













Instructions

1. Cook the Spaghetti
Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the spaghetti and cook according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
2. Prepare the Bolognese Sauce
Heat olive oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, and sauté until softened and fragrant, about 3-4 minutes.
3. Brown the Ground Beef
Add the ground beef to the pan and cook until browned, breaking it up with a spoon as it cooks. Drain any excess fat if necessary.
4. Add Tomatoes and Seasonings
Stir in the canned tomatoes, tomato paste, dried oregano, dried basil, sugar, salt, and pepper. Mix well and bring the sauce to a simmer. Let it cook for 20-25 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the sauce thickens.
5. Combine Spaghetti and Sauce
Add the cooked spaghetti to the pan with the Bolognese sauce. Toss gently to coat the spaghetti evenly with the sauce.
6. Serve
Serve the Spaghetti Bolognese hot, garnished with grated Parmesan cheese and fresh basil leaves. Enjoy!
