Instructions

1. Prepare the Veggies
Chop the bell peppers, onions, and spinach into small, bite-sized pieces.
2. Whisk the Eggs
In a bowl, whisk together the eggs, salt, and pepper until well combined and slightly frothy.
3. SautÃ© the Veggies
Heat olive oil or butter in a non-stick skillet over medium heat. Add the bell peppers and onions, and sautÃ© for 2-3 minutes until softened. Add the spinach and cook for another 1-2 minutes until wilted.
4. Pour in the Eggs
Pour the whisked eggs over the veggies in the skillet. Tilt the skillet to ensure the eggs spread evenly.
5. Add the Cheese
Once the eggs begin to set but are still slightly runny on top, sprinkle the shredded cheese over the omelette.
6. Fold and Cook
Carefully fold the omelette in half using a spatula. Cook for an additional 1-2 minutes, or until the cheese has melted and the eggs are fully cooked.
7. Serve
Slide the omelette onto a plate and serve hot. Garnish with additional herbs or veggies if desired.
