Instructions

1. Prepare the Apples
Peel, core, and slice the apples into thin wedges. Place them in a large mixing bowl and drizzle with lemon juice to prevent browning.
2. Make the Filling
Add the sugar, brown sugar, flour, cinnamon, nutmeg, and salt to the apples. Toss well to coat the apples evenly.
3. Prepare the Pie Crust
Roll out one of the pie crusts and place it in a pie dish. Add the apple mixture into the crust, and dot the top with butter.
4. Add the Top Crust
Roll out the second pie crust and place it over the apples. Trim and crimp the edges, then cut slits in the top crust to allow steam to escape.
5. Bake the Pie
Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C). Bake the pie for 45-50 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the filling is bubbly.
6. Cool and Serve
Allow the pie to cool for at least 30 minutes before serving. Enjoy warm with a scoop of vanilla ice cream or whipped cream!
