Classic Apple Pie

PREP TIME45 Minutes
COOK TIME1 Hour
Dessert

A timeless American dessert featuring a flaky, buttery crust filled with tender cinnamon-spiced apples. Perfectly balanced between sweet and tart, this pie is a crowd-pleaser for any occasion.

Instructions

1. Prepare the Dough
Step 1: Cut 1 cup (227g) of chilled butter into small cubes. In a large bowl, whisk together flour and salt. Use a pastry cutter to blend butter into the flour until pea-sized crumbs form.

Step 2: Gradually add 6-8 tbsp ice water, mixing until dough just comes together. Divide into two disks, wrap in plastic, and refrigerate for 1 hour.
2. Make the Filling
Step 1: Peel and slice apples into ¼-inch thick wedges. Toss with sugar, lemon juice, cinnamon, and 2 tbsp flour in a large bowl. Let sit for 15 minutes.
3. Assemble the Pie
Step 1: Roll out one dough disk into a 12-inch circle. Line a 9-inch pie dish, trimming excess edges. Add apple filling and dot with 2 tbsp butter.

Step 2: Roll out the second dough disk and cut into 1-inch strips. Weave strips over the filling in a lattice pattern. Crimp edges with a fork.
4. Bake
Step 1: Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C). Brush lattice with egg wash (1 egg + 1 tbsp water). Sprinkle with coarse sugar.

Step 2: Bake for 20 minutes, then reduce heat to 375°F (190°C) and bake 40-50 minutes until golden and bubbling. Cool completely before slicing.
