Indian Naan Bread

PREP TIME1 Hour
COOK TIME30 Minutes
Bread

Nutrition Information

Calories 250 kcal
Total Fat 6 g
Protein 7 g
Carbohydrate 40 g
Cholesterol 10 mg

Indian Naan Bread is a soft and fluffy flatbread that pairs perfectly with curries and stews. It is traditionally cooked in a tandoor but can be easily made on a skillet at home.

Ingredients









Instructions

1. Prepare the Dough
Mix flour, sugar, salt, and baking powder. Add warm milk, water, and yogurt, kneading until smooth. Let it rest for 1 hour.
2. Shape the Naan
Divide the dough into small balls and roll each into an oval shape.
3. Cook
Heat a skillet over medium heat. Cook each naan for about 2 minutes on one side, then flip and cook for another 1-2 minutes.
4. Serve
Brush with melted butter and serve warm.
