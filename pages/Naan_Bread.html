Indian Naan Bread

PREP TIME10 Minutes
COOK TIME5 Minutes
Side Dish

Naan is a soft and pillowy flatbread, popular in Indian cuisine. It is traditionally baked in a tandoor oven, but you can easily make it at home in a hot skillet. Serve it with curries, dips, or enjoy it on its own.

Instructions

1. Make the Dough
Combine flour, sugar, salt, and baking powder in a bowl. Add yogurt, water, and olive oil. Mix into a smooth dough.
2. Rest the Dough
Cover the dough with a damp cloth and let it rest for 1 hour.
3. Roll the Dough
Divide the dough into small balls and roll each into a flat disc, about 6-8 inches in diameter.
4. Cook the Naan
Heat a skillet over medium-high heat. Cook each naan for 1-2 minutes on each side until golden brown and slightly charred.
5. Serve the Naan
Brush the naan with melted butter and serve warm with curry or dips.
