Instructions

1. Preheat the Oven
Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C). Grease a donut pan.
2. Mix Dry Ingredients
In a bowl, whisk flour, sugar, baking powder, nutmeg, and salt.
3. Combine Wet Ingredients
In another bowl, mix milk, eggs, melted butter, and vanilla. Pour into dry ingredients and stir until just combined.
4. Fill Donut Pan
Spoon batter into a piping bag and pipe into the prepared donut pan, filling each cavity ¾ full.
5. Bake
Bake for 8-10 minutes until lightly golden. Let cool in the pan for 5 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack.
6. Make Glaze
Whisk powdered sugar, cocoa powder, milk, and vanilla until smooth. Dip each cooled donut into the glaze and let set.
