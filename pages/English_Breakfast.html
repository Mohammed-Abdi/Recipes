Instructions

1. Fry the Bacon
Heat a frying pan over medium heat. Add the bacon slices and cook for 3-4 minutes on each side, or until crispy. Remove from the pan and set aside.
2. Cook the Sausages
In the same pan, cook the sausages for 8-10 minutes, turning them occasionally until they are golden brown and cooked through. Set aside with the bacon.
3. Grill the Tomatoes
Place the halved tomatoes cut side down in the pan and cook for about 3 minutes, until softened and slightly charred. Remove from the pan and set aside.
4. Heat the Baked Beans
In a separate small pan, heat the canned baked beans over low heat until warmed through, about 5 minutes. Stir occasionally.
5. Toast the Bread
While the other ingredients cook, toast the slices of bread in a toaster or on a grill. Spread butter on the toasted bread once done.
6. Fry the Eggs
In the same pan used for the other ingredients, add a little olive oil and crack the eggs into the pan. Cook for 2-3 minutes for sunny-side-up eggs, or flip them for over-easy eggs. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
7. Assemble the Breakfast
Arrange the cooked sausages, bacon, grilled tomatoes, baked beans, fried eggs, and toast on a large plate. Serve immediately while hot.
