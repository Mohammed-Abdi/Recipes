Vegetable Curry

PREP TIME15 Minutes
COOK TIME30 Minutes
Main Course

Nutrition Information

Calories 250 kcal
Total Fat 15 g

Carbohydrate 25 g

This Vegetable Curry is a flavorful, hearty dish packed with a variety of vegetables and spices. The rich curry sauce brings together the best of seasonal veggies, making it a perfect meal for lunch or dinner. It's customizable with your favorite vegetables and serves as a delicious vegetarian option.

Instructions

1. Sauté the Aromatics
In a large pot, heat the vegetable oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion, garlic, and ginger. Sauté for 2-3 minutes until fragrant and softened.
2. Cook the Vegetables
Add the cubed potatoes, sliced carrots, chopped zucchini, and cauliflower florets to the pot. Stir well to combine with the aromatics.
3. Add Spices and Tomatoes
Sprinkle in the curry powder, cumin, turmeric, salt, and pepper. Stir to coat the vegetables in the spices, then add the diced tomatoes. Cook for 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.
4. Add the Coconut Milk
Pour in the coconut milk and stir everything together. Bring the mixture to a simmer, then reduce the heat and let it cook for 20 minutes, or until the vegetables are tender.
5. Adjust Seasonings
Taste the curry and adjust the seasoning with more salt, pepper, or spices if necessary.
6. Serve
Serve the vegetable curry hot with rice or naan. Garnish with fresh cilantro if desired.
