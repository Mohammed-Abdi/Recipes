Instructions

1. Prepare the Dough
In a large bowl, combine the flour, sugar, yeast, and salt. Add the warm milk and mix until a dough forms. Knead the dough on a floured surface for 5-7 minutes until smooth. Cover and let it rest for 1 hour.
2. Laminate the Dough
Roll out the dough into a large rectangle. Place the chilled butter in the center and fold the dough over it. Roll out the dough again and fold it into thirds. Repeat this process 3-4 times, chilling the dough for 30 minutes between folds.
3. Shape the Croissants
Roll out the dough into a large rectangle and cut it into triangles. Roll each triangle from the base to the tip to form a croissant shape. Place the croissants on a baking sheet and let them rise for 1 hour.
4. Bake the Croissants
Preheat the oven to 375Â°F (190Â°C). Brush the croissants with beaten egg and bake for 15-20 minutes, or until golden brown.
5. Serve
Let the croissants cool slightly before serving. Enjoy warm with butter, jam, or your favorite spread.
