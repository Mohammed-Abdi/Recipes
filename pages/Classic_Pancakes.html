Classic Pancakes

PREP TIME10 Minutes
COOK TIME10 Minutes
Breakfast

Nutrition Information

Total Fat 6 g

Carbohydrate 30 g

Classic Pancakes are fluffy, golden, and perfect for a hearty breakfast. Made with simple ingredients like flour, milk, and eggs, these pancakes are quick to prepare and delicious to eat. Serve them with maple syrup, fresh fruits, or your favorite toppings for a delightful morning treat!

Instructions

1. Prepare the Batter
In a large bowl, sift together the flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar. Make a well in the center and pour in the milk, egg, and melted butter. Mix until smooth.
2. Heat the Pan
Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium-high heat. Pour about 1/4 cup of batter onto the griddle for each pancake.
3. Cook the Pancakes
Cook until bubbles form on the surface and the edges look set, about 2-3 minutes. Flip and cook the other side until golden brown, about 1-2 minutes.
4. Serve
Serve the pancakes warm with your favorite toppings, such as maple syrup, fresh fruits, or whipped cream.
