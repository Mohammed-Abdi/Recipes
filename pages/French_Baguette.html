French Baguette

French Baguette is a classic, crusty bread with a soft and airy interior. Perfect for sandwiches, dipping in soups, or enjoying with butter, this bread is a staple in French cuisine. With just a few simple ingredients, you can create an authentic baguette at home.

Instructions

1. Prepare the Dough
In a large bowl, mix the flour, salt, and yeast. Gradually add the warm water and mix until a dough forms. Knead the dough for 10 minutes until smooth and elastic.
2. First Rise
Place the dough in a lightly oiled bowl, cover with a damp cloth, and let it rise for 2 hours, or until doubled in size.
3. Shape the Baguette
Divide the dough into two equal portions. Roll each portion into a long, thin baguette shape. Place the shaped dough on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
4. Second Rise
Cover the shaped dough with a damp cloth and let it rise for 1 hour.
5. Preheat the Oven
Preheat your oven to 450°F (230°C). Place a pan of water on the bottom rack to create steam.
6. Bake the Baguette
Score the top of the baguettes with a sharp knife. Bake for 20-25 minutes, or until golden brown and crusty.
7. Serve
Let the baguettes cool slightly before slicing. Serve warm with butter or your favorite spread.
