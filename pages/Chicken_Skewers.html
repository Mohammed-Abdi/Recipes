Grilled

These Chicken Skewers with Sweet Peppers are a perfect summer dish. The tender chicken, paired with the sweetness of the peppers, creates a delicious balance of flavors. Grilled to perfection, this dish is great for any cookout or family dinner.

Instructions

1. Prepare the Marinade
In a large bowl, combine the olive oil, paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper. Add the lemon juice and whisk everything together until smooth. This will form the flavorful marinade for the chicken and vegetables.
2. Marinate the Chicken
Cut the chicken breasts into cubes and add them to the marinade. Toss the chicken well to ensure every piece is coated evenly. Cover and refrigerate the chicken for at least 15 minutes, but up to 2 hours for more flavor.
3. Prepare the Skewers
While the chicken marinates, slice the sweet peppers into 1-inch strips. Soak wooden skewers in water for at least 30 minutes to prevent burning. If using metal skewers, no soaking is required. Alternate threading the marinated chicken and sweet pepper slices onto the skewers.
4. Preheat the Grill
Preheat your grill to medium-high heat (about 400Â°F or 200Â°C). This will allow the chicken to cook through while achieving a nice char on the peppers.
5. Grill the Skewers
Place the skewers on the grill and cook for about 5-7 minutes per side. Turn the skewers halfway through cooking to ensure that the chicken is cooked evenly. The internal temperature of the chicken should reach 165Â°F (75Â°C).
6. Serve the Skewers
Once the chicken is cooked through and slightly charred, remove the skewers from the grill. Let the skewers rest for a few minutes before serving. Enjoy with a side of rice, a fresh salad, or some flatbreads for a complete meal.
